  <h1>Impact of 50% U.S. Tariff on Indian Exports</h1>

  <p>
    In 2025, the United States imposed a 50% tariff on Indian goods, significantly affecting India's exports to the U.S. market.
    This article analyzes the estimated market share of key Indian export sectors in the U.S. after the tariff imposition,
    considering price competitiveness and consumer preferences.
  </p>

  <h2>Summary</h2>
  <p>
    The total Indian market share post-tariff is roughly 30% of what it was before the tariff, assuming it was around 50%+ before.
    Pharmaceuticals and Gems & Jewelry remain the strongest sectors due to less price sensitivity and strong brand presence.
    Textiles, marine products, and agricultural goods are most affected due to price sensitivity and easy substitution by other suppliers.
  </p>
